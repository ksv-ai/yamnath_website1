About Me

Shaping Nepal's Scientific Future

As a dedicated science educator and educational innovator, I am committed to revolutionizing science education in Nepal through modern technology and innovative teaching methodologies.

With over 25 years of experience, I have led initiatives that bridge the gap between traditional teaching and contemporary learning. As President of the Science Teachers' Association — Lumbini Province, I work to elevate the standard of science education nationwide.

My approach combines hands-on experimentation, digital learning tools, and real-world applications to make science engaging and accessible to every student across Nepal.
