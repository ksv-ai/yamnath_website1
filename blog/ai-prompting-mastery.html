Talk to Robots: Legendry AI Prompting

Imagine you have a magical genie that can write anything, solve any math problem, or explain how a black hole works. But there's a catch: the genie is a literal robot. It only does exactly what you say!

THE SECRET RULE

The "RCTF" Framework

This is the secret code used by AI professionals (and the smartest students) to get perfect answers every time. Instead of just asking a question, follow these 4 steps:

Step Meaning Example
Role Who is the AI? "Act as a Science Teacher"
Context What is the situation? "I am a Class 10 student in Nepal"
Task What must it do? "Explain the heart's function"
Format How should it look? "Use a bulleted list"

Case Study for Teachers: Automated Quiz Generation

Teachers, you don't have to spend hours writing quizzes anymore. Use this "Golden Prompt":

"Act as a Science Curriculum Expert. Based on the SEE Class 10 Physics Syllabus for Nepal, generate 5 challenging Multiple Choice Questions regarding 'Heat and Thermodynamics'. Include an answer key and explanations for each."

Bad vs Master Prompts

See how the RCTF rule changes everything!

❌ The "Lazy" Prompt

Write a summary of Physics Chapter 1.

Result: A boring, long essay that you might not even understand.

✅ The "Master" Prompt

You are a Science Coach. Summarize the 'Force and Motion' chapter for a Class 10 student. Focus on the formulas. Presentation: A simple table with 'Concept' and 'Formula' columns.

Result: A perfect, clean table that you can use to study for your exam!
